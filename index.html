About me

I am a 10th-grade student. I enjoy looking at scenery, which is one of my hobbies. Personally, I'm not very skilled in photography, but I love using photos to capture the beautiful views that I find appealing. I believe that this hobby of mine is closely related to my love for reading because I often experience a whole new world through the words in books. The real-life scenery and beautiful things always stimulate my imagination and creativity.

Personal goal
